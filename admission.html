Admissions Process

A simple, transparent process designed to help us get to know your child and family

1

Submit Application

October - February

Complete and submit the online application form or download the printable version.

2

Entrance Assessment

March - April

Students take age-appropriate entrance assessments to evaluate readiness.

3

Interview

April - May

Parent and student interview with our admissions team.

4

Admission Decision

May - June

Notification of admission decision and enrollment confirmation.

Admission Requirements

Required documents vary by grade level. Please ensure all documents are ready
before applying.

Nursery (Ages 3-5)

  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization record
  • Passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Parent/Guardian ID copy

Primary (Ages 6-11)

  • Previous school report card
  • Transfer certificate (if applicable)
  • Birth certificate
  • Immunization record
  • Passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Parent/Guardian ID copy

Secondary (Ages 12-17)

  • Primary school leaving certificate
  • Common entrance result
  • Previous school report cards
  • Transfer certificate
  • Birth certificate
  • Passport photographs (4 copies)
  • Parent/Guardian ID copy
